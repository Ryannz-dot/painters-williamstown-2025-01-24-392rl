Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical painting project take?

Most residential projects are completed within 3-5 days, depending on the scope. Larger projects may take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

What type of paints do you use?

We exclusively use premium, eco-friendly paints from leading manufacturers like Dulux and Taubmans, ensuring superior coverage and longevity.

Do you offer color consultation services?

Yes, our professional color consultants can help you choose the perfect palette for your space, considering lighting, architecture, and your personal style.

Are you fully insured?

Yes, we carry comprehensive insurance coverage including public liability and workers' compensation for your peace of mind.

What preparation work is included?

Our service includes complete surface preparation: cleaning, sanding, filling holes/cracks, and priming where necessary to ensure the best possible finish.
